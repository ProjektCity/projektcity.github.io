How do I set my preferences?
If you are in the European Economic Area and are visiting our website for the first time, you will see a banner from Google asking for your consent. You have the option of either selecting “Agree” or adjusting your settings under “Manage options”.
By clicking “Agree”, you consent to Google and its partners processing data for various purposes, including
  • Storing and accessing information on your device
  • Use of reduced data to select advertisements and content
  • Creating profiles for personalized advertising and content
  • Measuring advertising performance and the performance of content
  • Analysis of target groups through statistical evaluations
  • Combination of data from various sources to improve offers
  • Security assurance, fraud prevention and troubleshooting
  • Storage and transmission of your data protection decisions
  • Identification of end devices and linking of multiple devices
  • Use of precise location data
If you click on “Manage Options”, a menu will open in which you can customize the various purposes of use and allow or reject certain providers for advertising purposes.
If you deactivate a purpose of use, this means that your data will not be processed for this purpose by any provider.
At the bottom of the menu you will find the “Provider settings”, where you can view the providers that may use your data to provide services. If you reject a provider, this may mean that this provider will no longer use your data for its purposes.
Next to each provider, the type of consent they are asking for is displayed and you have the option to access the provider's privacy policy. You can give or withdraw your consent at any time. However, some providers do not ask for your consent, but process your personal data on the basis of a legitimate interest.
You will also find the options “Accept all” and “Confirm selection”. If you click on “Accept all”, you consent to Google and its partners using your data for the purposes mentioned above, among others.
If you click on “Confirm selection”, only the purposes and providers you have selected will be taken into account.
Further information on data processing can be found in the Google Privacy Policy and in our Privacy Policy.

You can customize your preferences for Google Ads at any time. To do this, click on the text “Privacy and cookie settings” at the bottom of a page and the menu mentioned above will open.

Neccessarry Cookies
Cookies are small text files that are sent to the user's browser, desktop client or device when they visit the website. These are automatically provided by the device when the page is visited again. Cookies are mostly used by websites to improve the user experience, increase the performance of the site and - if activated - display personalized content.
The law states that companies may automatically store necessary cookies on devices without user consent if they are necessary for the operation of the site.
For all other types of cookies, such as analytics and statistics, preferences and personalization, marketing, advertising and all other cookies, the user's consent is required.
You can change your cookie settings at any time. To do this, go to the bottom of one of our pages, click on "change cookie preferences" and select your preferences.
Statistics and Analytics
Statistics and analysis cookies, also known as "performance cookies," help the website to track how users interact with the site, which link is clicked by them, how long they visit it, which pages are particularly popular and which pages the users come from. This information is collected anonymously and is not trackable and helps us to better understand our site and users and their behavior in order to provide a better experience.
Preferences and Personalization
Preference cookies, are cookies that allow the website to remember your choices you made through your use of our website, such as language and regional preferences, or what your username and password are so that they can be retrieved automatically the next time you load the page, leading to a overall better performance of the webpage.
Third-party content
Third-party cookies include embedded videos, widgets, images or other types of third-party content. If this feature is activated, a connection to other servers is established and external content is loaded. Some content may not be created or managed by Projekt City. This feature is disabled by default to protect your privacy.
Marketing and Advertisement
Marketing cookies are mainly used for advertising purposes by us and our third parties. If this function is activated, your online activities are tracked in order to provide you with personalized, relevant, meaningfull and appropriate advertising and to prevent you from seeing the same advertising multiple times. This can help us track how efficient advertising campaigns are, both for our own services and other websites. All data is passed on anonymously to the advertising partners.
Others & unclassified cookies
Unclassified cookies are cookies that cannot be assigned to a specific category or class, either because they are too irrelevant or not worth to categorize. Nevertheless, we are working on classifying all cookies as best we can.
