Neccessarry Cookies
Cookies are small text files that are sent to the user's browser, desktop client or device when they visit the website. These are automatically provided by the device when the page is visited again. Cookies are mostly used by websites to improve the user experience, increase the performance of the site and - if activated - display personalized content.
The law states that companies may automatically store necessary cookies on devices without user consent if they are necessary for the operation of the site.
For all other types of cookies, such as analytics and statistics, preferences and personalization, marketing, advertising and all other cookies, the user's consent is required.
You can change your cookie settings at any time. To do this, go to the bottom of one of our pages, click on "change cookie preferences" and select your preferences.
