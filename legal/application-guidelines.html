Application Guidelines

Projekt City Ltd. is a free project and the provider of this service. Subject to the terms of this agreement, this service allows you to download a (ZIP compressed) file to your computer at your own risk, which you can load, run and use/play in Minecraft. Please read this agreement (“agreement”, “guidelines”, “terms”, “policy”) carefully and thoroughly, as it explains how, for what, under what conditions and with what restrictions you can/may use the services of Projekt City Ltd (“Projekt City”, “Projekt City Ltd.”, “we”, “us”, “our”) and how we use and process your data.
If you do not agree to our Advertisement Terms, Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, and Use Our World Policy, we must ask you to leave our websites and refrain from using our services!
#1 Applying as builder
1.1
You must be at least 14 years old to apply as a Builder. You must also be spiritually mature and appreciate the value/effort of this project to join us. Depending on your background, the age may vary, or we may not accept you at all.
1.2
We reserve the right to refuse or ignore any request without reason. For example, if we have enough builders, do not currently have time to accept builders or for other reasons. You must accept this and not enter into any discussions with us. We may also reject some people for reasons such as their age, spiritual maturity, insufficient knowledge, etc.
1.3
You may have to go through a certain process before we accept you/your request. This process can always vary and we do not have a specific process for this. You are required to complete this process unless we tell you otherwise. Otherwise we will not be able to accept you into our team.
1.4
You have a duty to always listen to us and not to do any other activities on our world that we have not assigned to you. You also have a duty to always inform us of any problems or errors and to keep us up to date at all times.
#2 Privacy
2.1
The most important thing for us is to protect you and your data and to handle it in a trustworthy manner. We treat your data with care and will never pass it on to third parties. Some information may have to be provided voluntarily.
2.2
By applying, you agree that data collected during your stay with us will only be used for legitimate purposes. For example, if we discover a breach of the law, we will use your data to contact you or your legal guardian (if you are not over 18).
2.3
Your information is safe with us and will never be shared if necessary. We treat your data with care and will not use it for any purpose other than to contact you.
2.4
We store your data online on our own servers, to which only we and no other persons/companies have access. We also encrypt your data so that no one can open it without a password.
2.5
If you leave this project, we will keep your data for one week and then delete it from all our servers/networks as we no longer need it. We will also delete any other information about you related to this project. In addition, your Minecraft name will be removed from our website, but not from the downloadable documents (we put all information about our project in the world download folder with every update).
2.6
We treat your data with care so that you can feel safe with us and don't have to worry about your data being made public and you can therefore work "privately".
#3 Rights
3.1
You have the right to leave this project at any time or to pause your work. We do not want to force anyone to work with us if they do not want to, as we stand for a pleasant working environment. If you have any questions or other issues, you can also use our Discord server to talk to other employees.
3.2
We have the right to remove you from this project at any time if we discover misconduct. We tell you what to do, and you have to do it!
#4 Leaving this project
4.1
Before you leave this project, you must be aware that you have correctly completed all your outstanding projects. In addition, you must let us know that you will be leaving this project so that we can prepare for this.
4.2
Since by joining this project you transfer your built items to us and they become our property, you do not have the right to have your built items removed. However, we can remove your name from the credits on our site if you wish.
#5 Consent to publication
5.1
By joining this project, you agree that you consent to us mentioning your Minecraft name on our website(s).
5.2
You give us the copyright to your built items so that we can decide what happens to your items so that they become our property. You therefore no longer have the right to request the deletion of your built items.
#6 Contact and Communication
6.1
The Builder assures that he/she provides a current and genuine e-mail address and checks it regularly. This e-mail address will be used as a means of communication.
6.2
We have the right to contact you at any time via this e-mail address and you are obliged to respond to our e-mails (if necessary) correctly, accurately and quickly. Otherwise, we may remove you from our team as mentioned in 1.2.
6.3
If you change your contact details, you must inform us so that we can update them. These must meet the requirements mentioned in 6.1 and 6.2.
#7 Fees
7.1
The Client acknowledges that fees may apply for certain services. These fees and payment terms will be stated on the Forum or in separate communications.
7.2
Payment of Fees shall be made in accordance with the payment terms set out on the Forum. The Builder is responsible for timely payment.
