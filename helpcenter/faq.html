Frequently Asked Questions

Am I allowed to use the world on a public server?
Yes, you may use our world on servers, but you must draw attention to our project and inform us about it.
Further information about how you are allowed to use our world can you find in our Use Our World Policy
Does it cost money to play the world?   
No, the world is completely free to play, you only need to pay for Minecraft, then you can play the world.
If Projekt City is free? How does it finance itself?
It does not. Projekt City is just a Hobby of me and SuffixHD. We only develop it in our free time and want to learn new things from it. We do not rely on money from this project, even though CurseForge is earning a few cents every month.
On which platforms can you also find Projekt City?
You can find Projekt City on Instagram, Threads, TikTok and YouTube. And more platforms will follow! You can find our social media channels at https://bit.ly/ProjektCityLinktree
TIP: You can click on the symbol in the navigation bar to go directly to our LinkTree without having to enter the link or visit the Helpcenter.
I have found an error, what do I do?
First of all, thank you for finding this bug. You can find the form to report bugs under the following adress: https://forms.office.com/r/3W4NYS3yaN
Open the link, fill out the form as precise as you can and we will take care of the bug.
If we need further information, we will reach out to you.
When will there be a new update for the map?
Our biggest update V5.0 is out now and is waiting to be downloaded by you!
You can download it under https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/worlds/projekt-city/files/6043384
How do I use the navigation bar?
Information: The navbar is always avaible on every site, which you visit within Projekt City!
Using the navigation bar is as simple as you can imagine it:
In the left you can see our logo and a scrolling text. When you click on that, you will always go back to our mainpage.
In the middle you can see our most important pages, which we think users might need the most. Click on one of the texts to open the site.
On the right you can see three symbols. These are the most important links to other pages, which are not within the Projekt City page.
When you click on the symbol, you will be taken to our CurseForge site for more information about our project
When you click on the symbol, you will be redirected to our Helpcenter, which will hopefully give you the information you need.
When you click on the symbol, you will be redirected to our LinkTree in which you can find all our social media channels and important links.

Neccessarry Cookies
Cookies are small text files that are sent to the user's browser, desktop client or device when they visit the website. These are automatically provided by the device when the page is visited again. Cookies are mostly used by websites to improve the user experience, increase the performance of the site and - if activated - display personalized content.
The law states that companies may automatically store necessary cookies on devices without user consent if they are necessary for the operation of the site.
For all other types of cookies, such as analytics and statistics, preferences and personalization, marketing, advertising and all other cookies, the user's consent is required.
You can change your cookie settings at any time. To do this, go to the bottom of one of our pages, click on "change cookie preferences" and select your preferences.
Statistics and Analytics
Statistics and analysis cookies, also known as "performance cookies," help the website to track how users interact with the site, which link is clicked by them, how long they visit it, which pages are particularly popular and which pages the users come from. This information is collected anonymously and is not trackable and helps us to better understand our site and users and their behavior in order to provide a better experience.
Preferences and Personalization
Preference cookies, are cookies that allow the website to remember your choices you made through your use of our website, such as language and regional preferences, or what your username and password are so that they can be retrieved automatically the next time you load the page, leading to a overall better performance of the webpage.
Third-party content
Third-party cookies include embedded videos, widgets, images or other types of third-party content. If this feature is activated, a connection to other servers is established and external content is loaded. Some content may not be created or managed by Projekt City. This feature is disabled by default to protect your privacy.

Marketing cookies are mainly used for advertising purposes by us and our third parties. If this function is activated, your online activities are tracked in order to provide you with personalized, relevant, meaningfull and appropriate advertising and to prevent you from seeing the same advertising multiple times.
