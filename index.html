Projekt City

by SuffixHD & itsmarian

Projekt City is a unique and expansive Minecraft project that operates without funding and is entirely free to play for everyone. Our mission is to develop an easily navigable Minecraft world for everyone, while paying a lot of attention to details and flawless work. Within "Projekt City," we are constructing a large map that features a city, an airport, a hotel, and much more, with meticulous attention to detail and precision. We enhance the gameplay through intricate redstone creations and commands.

The Projekt City map spans 1,164 meters in width and 1,833 meters in length, covering a total area of 2,133,612 square meters*, making it a very large Minecraft project! Our current efforts are focused on developing the airport and hotel, which occupy a significant portion of the map.

All versions after 4.1 are in English and will remain so until the project's completion, as adding other languages is a significant undertaking. We plan to introduce additional languages, such as German, French, and Italian, after completing the project to enhance accessibility for more players.

To provide detailed information, we have launched a website where players can find essential details and ask questions. We strive to address player requests swiftly and professionally, aiming to create a bug-free world. We encourage you to report any bugs or errors on our Discord server or forum. We are committed to enhancing our professionalism and skills in building, redstone, and commands to becom to become more professional, more accurate and better!


Thank you for your support,

SuffixHD & itsmarian

Install with Python

You can easily install Projekt City automatically and 100% securely with Python (supports only Windows):
#1
Install python for your operating system: https://www.python.org/downloads/
#2
Open the command line and install the following libraries:
pip install requests
pip install tqdm
#3
Download the Python installer for Projekt City
#4
Open your file explorer and open the file.
#5
Accept the terms and conditions [Y] and select the version you want to download and press [ENTER]
Now the script will download the file from the servers of CurseForge, extract it and move it in your Minecraft folder.
When the process is finished, you will see a message.

FAQ

Am I allowed to use the world on a public server?
Yes, you may use our world on servers, but you must draw attention to our project and inform us about it.
Further information about how you are allowed to use our world can you find in our Use Our World Policy
Does it cost money to play the world?   
No, Projekt City is completely free to play. You still need to own Minecraft before playing to be able to play the world.
I have found an error, what do I do?
First of all, thank you for finding this bug. You can find the form to report bugs under the following adress: https://forms.office.com/r/3W4NYS3yaN
Open the link, fill out the form as precise as you can and we will take care of the bug.
If we need further information, we will reach out to you.
On which platforms can you also find Projekt City?
You can find Projekt City on Instagram, Threads, TikTok and YouTube. And more platforms will follow! You can find our social media channels at https://bit.ly/ProjektCityLinktree
When will there be a new update for the map?

You can download it under https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/worlds/projekt-city/files/6043384
