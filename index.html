Projekt City

by SuffixHD & itsmarian


The Projekt City map spans 1,164 meters in width and 1,833 meters in length, covering a total area of 2,133,612 square meters, making it a very large Minecraft project! Our current efforts are focused on developing the airport and hotel, which occupy a significant portion of the map.

All versions after 4.1 are in English and will remain so until the project's completion, as adding other languages is a significant undertaking. We plan to introduce additional languages, such as German, French, and Italian, after completing the project to enhance accessibility for more players.

To provide detailed information, we have launched a website where players can find essential details and ask questions. We strive to address player requests swiftly and professionally, aiming to create a bug-free world. We encourage you to report any bugs or errors on our Discord server or forum. We are committed to enhancing our professionalism and skills in building, redstone, and commands to become more professional, more accurate and better!


Thank you for your support,

SuffixHD & itsmarian

Am I allowed to use the world on a public server?

Yes, you may use our world on servers, but you must draw attention to our project and inform us about it. Further information about how you are allowed to use our world can you find in our Use Our World Policy

Does it cost money to play the world?

No, Projekt City is completely free to play. You still need to own Minecraft before playing to be able to play the world.

I have found an error, what do I do?

First of all, thank you for finding this bug. You can find the form to report bugs under the following address: Report a bug. Open the link, fill out the form as precise as you can and we will take care of the bug. If we need further information, we will reach out to you.
